Why Choose Our Mini Bootcamp?

Investing $15,000 in a full-scale coding bootcamp can be a big leap, especially if you're unsure about your passion for coding. Our 3-week Mini Coding Bootcamp, priced at just $170, offers the perfect opportunity to explore the world of programming and develop essential skills. By laying a solid foundation, you'll be better prepared to tackle a larger bootcamp with confidence, knowing it's the right choice for your future.
